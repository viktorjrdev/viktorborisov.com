The Geometry of Absolute Compliance.

I do not provide theoretical overviews; I engineer deployable legal structures. Below is a detailed breakdown of how each core methodology is executed from inception to certification.
01

ISO 27001 Internal Audit

Validating the integrity of Information Security Management Systems (ISMS).

Achieving ISO 27001 is a grueling gauntlet. I conduct hyper-rigorous internal audits designed to aggressively uncover non-conformities *before* the external certification body does. The result is a seamless final audit and an impenetrable data defense network.

  • Phase I: The Gap AssessmentMapping your current infrastructure against the Annex A controls.
  • Phase II: Critical PenetrationSimulating risk to expose documentation and operational flaws.
  • Phase III: Board-Level ReportingDelivering an actionable matrix for immediate executive remediation.
02

Policy Architecture & SOPs

Drafting the algorithmic foundation of corporate governance.

Regulatory compliance fails when policies are written but not executed. I architect Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) that translate complex multinational laws into explicit, executable steps for your lower-level workforce.

  • Phase I: Regulatory MappingIsolating applicable cross-border jurisdictional requirements.
  • Phase II: Framework DraftingWriting airtight policies (e.g., AML, KYC, GDPR, Data Retention).
  • Phase III: OperationalizationConverting legal text into rigid step-by-step procedures for staff.
03

High-Conflict Mediation

Neutralizing destructive institutional disputes.

Litigation is public, expensive, and protracted. Certified Mediation is confidential, efficient, and precise. I deploy strategic outcome-mapping to de-escalate emotional warfare between partners, executives, or B2B actors.

  • Phase I: Forensic Fact-FindingExtracting objective truths from emotional narratives.
  • Phase II: Interest DecouplingIsolating the personal grievances from the financial necessities.
  • Phase III: Binding ResolutionDrafting the final, legally binding settlement agreement.
04

Administrative Legal Advocacy

Wielding the law against bureaucratic inertia.

When government agencies or institutional oversight bodies step over the line or fail to act, aggressive documentation is required. I draft dense, intimidating, and procedurally flawless administrative complaints to force action and protect corporate interests.

  • Phase I: Procedural ExploitationAnalyzing the target agency's own bylaws for leverage points.
  • Phase II: Substantive DraftingConstructing the multi-page complaint with undeniable evidentiary backing.
  • Phase III: Escalation StrategyManaging the appellate process until standard procedures are rectified.
